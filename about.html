ITER AD ASTRA

ITER AD ASTRA, the title of this mini webpage, is a Latin phrase which translates to "Path to the Stars". The contributors chose this title since the webpage shows the journey of Mr. Saturnino G. Belen on establishing FAITH Colleges through illustrations and games made by the contributors.

MORE FROM US

This webpage talks about the Anecdotal History of FAITH Colleges and how to be a better First Asian. Attached here on the webpage is also a game related to the history developed by some of the contributors.

We initially though of making a comics to show the anecdotal history but thought that since we are all future software and game developers, we related our project on our programs.

The objective of this webpage is to share the story and tips of First Asians to the users of the webpage through an interesting way. We thought of making a project that will be interactive and exciting for the users.

As for the logo, the two small stars stands for Mr. Belen's experience on establishing the largest star, FAITH Colleges. The lines serves as the path taken by SGB upon establising the institution.
